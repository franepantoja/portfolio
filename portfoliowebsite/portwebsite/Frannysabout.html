Hello!
My name is Francesca Pantoja. I was born in Chicago, IL and raised in Kenosha, WI. My interest for art started at a young age by just drawing and making crafts in grade school. Later on, I discovered how you could take physical art and create it through technology by my father who is a graphic designer, thus my interest in digital design began. I currently attend UW-Parkside as a Graphic Design major. I spend my time as a full-time student, while also holding a part time job and freelancing on the side. Expected to graduate Fall of 2023.
